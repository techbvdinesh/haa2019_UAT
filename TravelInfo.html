WELCOME TO TORONTO, CANADA  - Facts you may note

Toronto: One of the best destinations in North America
GTA Created most High Tech jobs in North America in the last 5 years –surprised? Yes, this is true
Toronto and area is a great place to visit, vacation, relax and do site-seeing. Within driving distance from the US East Coast cities and the Midwest and conveniently accessible by air, Toronto is much closer than you think. There are many international airports including the BEST NORTH AMERICAN LARGE AIRPORT at Toronto Pearson. US travelers may find it economical to fly to Buffalo or Detroit and
drive from there. Canadian Dollar is currently around 75 Cents US and hence US Dollar will go much farther here. 

OH CANADA
Untamed, Refined, Culturally Captivating
from Sea to Sea to Sea

For those who are visiting Canada for the first time, here are some key facts about Toronto and Canada:
  • LANGUAGE- Primarily English; plus French and many other ethnic languages spoken at different neighborhoods
  • DRIVING- Same as United States – on the right side of the road,Highway Speed Limit: 100 km/h (62 mph); Roads: varies 40 km/h to 60 km/h
  • CURRENCY - Canadian Dollar -a major advantage to US residents dollar will be increases by about 25% to 30% (varies)
  • MEASUREMENTS - Kilometers (1 km = 0.62 miles); Kilograms (1 kg =2.2 lbs)
  • MONEY TRANSACTIONS - All major credit cards, plenty of ATMS, International Banks

Visa Details

  1. US Citizens  - MUST bring your Passport to cross the border. 
  2. US Green card holders - Passport & ETA (Electronic Travel Form) if you are flying-in; ETA not required for land crossing.  
  3. US VISA Holders - (J-1, H-1, Visitor etc.) –apply for Canadian Visitor VISA and Electronic Travel Form (ETA) BEFORE you travel.  Humorous items (specifically Havyaka havyasa type) are welcome.For details please visit Canada Visistor Visa

NOTE: CANADA DOES NOT ISSUE TRAVEL/VISITOR VISA AT THE BORDER OR AIRPORTS. Get the VISA well before Travel-Don’t Be disappointed.

DO NOT BRING: Guns, street drugs, fresh fruits & vegetables. – Important.

Travel Details

Travel By Road from United States

Toronto is at an easy driving distance from many parts of United States.
1 . Within 9 hrs drive from: Boston; New York City, New Jersey, Washington DC;Philadelphia, and Chicago.
2. Other travel times are Pittsburgh (6 hrs), Cleveland (5 hrs), Columbus (6 hrs)

Travel by AIR

Many International and Regional airports:
Major Airports in Toronto:
1. Toronto Pearson International
2. Toronto Billy Bishop City Airport;
Other nearby airports are: 
Hamilton (1 hr drive); Kitchener (1 hr. ); Buffalo US (2 hrs ); Detroit US (4 hrs );
Note: Flying to Buffalo or Detroit and renting a car may be more economical in some cases.

Sports

Toronto is home for many Major league Sports: Blue Jays Baseball, Toronto Raptors Basket Ball; Toronto FC Soccer, Maple Leaf Hockey etc.
Tickets are always sold out-plan in advance if interested.

Population/Demography

Toronto (“Meeting Place” in aboriginal language) is an exciting multi-cultural city of more than 5 million people in Canada just across the border from United States. US residents will find it very attractive, clean, safe, economical (75 cents to the US dollar) and an extension of US with some interesting differences. 

Toronto is one of the most diverse Cities in the world. More than 50% of Toronto population have different ethnic origin. Many many different neighborhoods thrive and prosper in the Greater Toronto area -India Town(s), China Town(s), Greek Town, Italian, Korean, Farsi etc. East Indians including Sri Lankan, Pakistani and Bangladeshi origin people make up more than 1 million people in GTA.
